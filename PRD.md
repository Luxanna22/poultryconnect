🧠 1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW
🎯 Purpose

PoultryConnect 2.0 is a web-based platform designed to help smallholder poultry farmers:

monitor farm operations
analyze production data
make informed decisions
connect with buyers and stakeholders
💡 Problem Statement

Farmers rely on:

manual record-keeping
unstable pricing decisions
lack of market access

👉 Result:

low productivity
income uncertainty
poor decision-making
✅ Solution

A centralized system that:

collects farm data
analyzes it using predictive logic
provides insights and recommendations
connects farmers to buyers
👥 2. TARGET USERS
🐔 Farmer / Owner
monitors farm performance
makes decisions
manages marketplace
👨‍🌾 Staff
inputs daily farm data
ensures accurate records
🛒 Buyer / Customer
browses and purchases products
tracks orders
